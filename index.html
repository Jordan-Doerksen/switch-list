How to play & why these rules

You are the conductor, not the engineer. Click a switch target to line it (yellow = lined into the track / diverging, green = normal, straight up the ladder). Then call a move:

  • PULL n from a track — back in, couple the near (throat) cut, pull n cars onto the lead.
  • SPOT n to a track — shove n cars in and leave them.

Score = fewest MOVES (one PULL or SPOT = one move) — beat or match par. Joints (couplings) is a second, "also good" stat: lower is cleaner, but you never add a move just to save a coupling. To reach a track, line its switch reverse and the ones below it normal, or the engineer refuses (CROR 104).
